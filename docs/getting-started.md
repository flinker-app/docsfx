## Recommended rollout sequence

1. **Review data handling**  
   Confirm where IFC files, project documents, and technical metadata are processed. Start with [Architecture and data protection](ifc-viewer-architecture-and-data-protection.md).

2. **Choose the Microsoft 365 surface**  
   Decide whether your users need SharePoint pages, Teams tabs, Power BI reports, Excel workflows, or a custom SDK integration.

3. **Identify the administrator role**  
   Use the [Admin deployment overview](it-admin-quickstart.md) to determine whether the deployment requires a SharePoint Administrator, Teams Administrator, Fabric Administrator, Power BI Administrator, Global Administrator, or Application Administrator.

4. **Run a controlled pilot**  
   Test with representative IFC files, permission groups, and business users before broad rollout.

5. **Publish the workflow**  
   Roll out the approved solution, document ownership, and link users to the relevant product guide and FAQ.
